# SwingElo

Analytics and scoring for **West Coast Swing** competitions.

SwingElo tracks competition results across the WCS circuit — from newcomer prelims to champion finals — and turns them into meaningful metrics. We score dancers, rate judges, calculate odds, and provide tools to understand your competitive trajectory.

:::note
Currently tracking **176 events**, **13,684 dancers**, and **175,665 competition appearances** spanning 2018–2026.
:::

## What you'll find here

- **How we score things** — Transparent methodology for every metric on the site
- **What the numbers mean** — How to read your profile, callback stats, and rankings
- **Competition data** — What we track, where it comes from, and how divisions work

## Key metrics

### Strength Score
A 0–100 composite rating based on your competition performance. Uses different weights depending on your level — novice dancers are scored on callback quality and advancement, while higher-level dancers are scored more heavily on finals placement and WSDC data.

### Odds to Finals
Your estimated probability of making finals at each event, based on your strength relative to historical competitors at that event.

### Power Rankings
A ranked list of the top dancers in the circuit, combining finals performance, callback rates, WSDC points, activity, and longevity.

### Judge Quality
Which judges are best at identifying talent early — specifically, which judges give high marks to dancers who later advance to higher divisions.
